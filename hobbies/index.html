My name is Xuan Hiep and I am a nature photographer

Xuan Hiep is a nature photographer based in Hanoi, Vientam. Xuan Hieps focus is single physical beings, such as an animal or a flower. Because each photo is of one single item, the visual focus is the items composition.

His photographs of animals center around the materials colors, and textures that the animal is composed of. Photographs of flowers emphasize their physical structure, colors, and positioning.

Xuan Hiep travels around the world to specific countries whose ecosystems he wants to explore and photograph.
