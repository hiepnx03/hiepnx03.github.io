My name is Ngoc Vu and I am a nature photographer

Ngoc Vu is a nature photographer based in Hanoi, Vientam. Ngoc Vu’s focus is single physical beings, such as an animal or a flower. Because each photo is of one single item, the visual focus is the item’s composition.

His photographs of animals center around the materials colors, and textures that the animal is composed of. Photographs of flowers emphasize their physical structure, colors, and positioning.

Ngoc Vu travels around the world to specific countries whose ecosystems he wants to explore and photograph.
